Graphical Processing Unit

The graphics processing unit (GPU) of a computer often refered to as a graphics card or video card. Every computer uses a GPU to render images, video and 2D or 3D animations for display. The GPU performs calculations and frees up the CPU to do its tasks. There are two types of GPUs: integrated or discrete. Right now the two most popular manufacturer for GPUs are AMD and nVidia where each has there own ranges of performances and software.
