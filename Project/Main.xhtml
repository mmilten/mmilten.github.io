Inside of a computer

Computers are complicated devices that is used by everyone around the world. The inside of a computer is what makes it a powerful device that is able to do what it needs to do. Each part of a computer has its purpose and is carefully put together to form what we call a computer today. Many enthusist around the world build their own personal computer through buying the parts and putting it together themselves or with the help of third party sellers who build it for them. While this may seem daunting to the average person, building your own computer is not that difficult.

Here in PC World we will go over the general steps taken to build your own computer and what to look for when choosing the components of your computer. We've divided up the components into seperate categories: The case, motherboard, graphical processing unit (GPU), central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage, and power supply.
