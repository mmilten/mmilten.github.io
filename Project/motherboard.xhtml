Motherboard

The motherboard is a printed circut board and the base and foundation of a computer. Motherboards in a computer holds are the components of a system such as the GPU, CPU memory, and also the connections for all the peripherals. The motherboard allocates all the power to the system and allows communication between the all the componnents of a system. There are a variety of types of motherboards which are designed to fit different types and sizes of computers. Each of these are designed to work with a specific type of processeros and memmory There is no set standard on the many conenctions, ports, or expansion slots are on a motherboard. When choosing a motherboard one must look at the design specifications to determine the number of avaliable connections a motherboard allows.

Form Factor

Motherboards have a variety of form factors which each having differing amount of connections. Most modern motherboards come in three sizes.

  • ATX
  • Micro-ATX
  • Mini-ITX
